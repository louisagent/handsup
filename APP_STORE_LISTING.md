### Description (max 4,000 chars)

```
🙌 Be there. We'll film it.

You're at the best set of the year. Tame Impala. Laneway. The lights, the crowd, the drop you've been waiting for.

And then you pull out your phone.

Handsup exists so you don't have to choose between experiencing the moment and having the memory.

HOW IT WORKS

Someone at the show films it. They upload it to Handsup, tagged with the artist, festival, location, and date. You search for it after. You download it. It's yours.

No watermark. No paywall. Just the clip on your camera roll, ready to share.

SEARCH THE WAY YOU REMEMBER IT

Search by artist name, festival, city, or date. Find the exact set, the exact stage, the exact moment. Not an algorithm — your intent, instantly.

DOWNLOAD ANYTHING, KEEP IT FOREVER

Every clip you find can be saved directly to your camera roll. Full quality. No app required to share it anywhere after that.

UPLOAD. GET RECOGNISED.

If you do film something great, upload it and tag it properly. The best uploaders earn rewards — credits, merch, and festival tickets from our partners.

BUILT FOR FESTIVAL CULTURE

Handsup is designed around how festivals actually work. Browse by event, filter by location, discover what's happening near you. See upcoming festivals, find clips from ones you've already been to.

IT'S FREE

Upload free. Download free. The platform grows with the community.

---

Handsup is built in Melbourne, Australia. 💜
Made for the people who actually go to shows.
```
