# Handsup — Festival Outreach Contacts
> Research compiled March 2026. Verify before sending — contacts change seasonally.

---

## 🇦🇺 Australia — Tier 1 (Approach First)

### Laneway Festival
- **Why first:** Boutique, community-driven, independent spirit. Perfect brand alignment.
- **Attendance:** ~15,000 per city
- **Cities:** Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Adelaide, Perth, Fremantle
- **Contact:** partnerships@lanewayfestival.com *(verify on lanewayfestival.com)*
- **Social:** @lanewayfestival
- **Best timing:** Oct–Dec (pre-season planning for Feb event)
- **Note:** They've historically been open to innovative community partnerships

### Meredith Music Festival
- **Why:** "No dickheads" ethos is perfectly aligned with Handsup's presence-first philosophy. Fiercely loyal crowd.
- **Attendance:** ~10,000
- **Location:** Meredith, Victoria (Dec annually)
- **Contact:** info@meredithmusicfestival.com.au
- **Social:** @meredithmusicfest
- **Best timing:** Aug–Oct
- **Note:** Family-run, tight-knit team. Personal approach will land better than corporate pitch.

### Strawberry Fields Festival
- **Why:** Cult following, electronic music focus, young digitally-native audience
- **Attendance:** ~5,000
- **Location:** Tocumwal, NSW (Nov annually)
- **Contact:** Via contact form at strawberryfieldsfestival.com
- **Social:** @strawberryfieldsfest
- **Best timing:** Jul–Sep

### Field Day
- **Why:** Sydney NYD institution, massive electronic music crowd, very social-savvy audience
- **Attendance:** ~20,000
- **Location:** The Domain, Sydney (Jan 1 annually)
- **Contact:** info@fieldday.com.au *(verify)*
- **Social:** @fieldday
- **Best timing:** Sep–Nov

### Splendour in the Grass
- **Why:** Australia's biggest boutique festival. Getting them = massive credibility + content library overnight.
- **Attendance:** ~35,000
- **Location:** Byron Bay, NSW (July)
- **Contact:** Via contact form at splendourinthegrass.com (larger org — expect longer lead time)
- **Social:** @splendourinthegrass
- **Best timing:** Jan–Mar (8+ months before event)
- **Note:** Larger team, more formal process. Nail the smaller ones first, use them as social proof.

### Pitch Music & Arts
- **Why:** Premium boutique, art + music crossover, highly engaged community
- **Attendance:** ~5,000
- **Location:** Hargraves, Victoria (March)
- **Contact:** hello@pitchfestival.com.au *(verify)*
- **Social:** @pitch_festival
- **Best timing:** Oct–Dec

### Beyond the Valley
- **Why:** NYE festival, young crowd, large social following, electronic focus
- **Attendance:** ~10,000
- **Location:** Phillip Island area, VIC (Dec 28–Jan 1)
- **Contact:** info@beyondthevalley.com.au *(verify)*
- **Social:** @beyondthevalley
- **Best timing:** Aug–Oct

## 🎙️ Music Podcast / Creator Outreach

Target Australian music content creators on TikTok and Instagram who cover festivals:
- Search: #LanewayFestival, #Splendour, #AustralianFestival, #LiveMusicAustralia
- Look for accounts with 5K–100K followers — micro-influencers convert better
- DM with: "You're always at the festivals we're building for — want early access to Handsup?"
