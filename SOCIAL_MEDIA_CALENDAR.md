# Handsup — 30-Day Social Media Calendar
> TikTok + Instagram Reels | Launch Month

**Posting frequency:** 5x per week (Mon–Fri)
**Platforms:** TikTok (primary), Instagram Reels (repurpose same content)
**Tone:** Funny, self-aware, festival-native. Never corporate.
**The core irony to lean into:** An app that tells you to stop using your phone — marketed on TikTok.

---

## Week 1 — Problem Awareness
*Hook: make people feel seen about their phone behaviour at shows*

**Day 1 — Monday**
🎬 Format: Talking head / vlog style
📝 Hook: *"POV: You just watched an entire Tame Impala set through your phone screen"*
💡 Content: Recreate the experience — phone up blocking the view, shaky footage, the person behind you annoyed. "We built something so you never have to do this again."
🏷️ Tags: #festival #livemusic #tameimpala #phonefreeconcert #handsup

**Day 2 — Tuesday**
🎬 Format: Split screen
📝 Hook: *"Two types of people at every festival 👀"*
💡 Content: Left side — phone up the whole time filming. Right side — hands in the air, fully in it. "Which one are you?" CTA: Handsup lets you be the one on the right.
🏷️ Tags: #festivallife #concert #livemusic #handsup

**Day 3 — Wednesday**
🎬 Format: Text-on-screen / trend audio
📝 Hook: *"Things that live in my camera roll that I've never watched once"*
💡 Content: List format — dark shaky concert footage, blurry stage shots, 4-second clips of nothing. "All from festivals. Unwatched. Someone else had the good shot."
🏷️ Tags: #relatable #festival #concert #fypシ

**Day 4 — Thursday**
🎬 Format: React / commentary
📝 Hook: *"I asked 10 people at a festival if they'd watched the footage they filmed. The answers were brutal."*
💡 Content: Man-on-the-street style. Nobody has watched their footage. Punchline: "So why are we filming?"
🏷️ Tags: #festival #livemusic #experiment #foryou

**Day 5 — Friday**
🎬 Format: Aesthetic / vibe reel
📝 Hook: *"What it actually looks like when no one's filming"*
💡 Content: Beautiful slow-mo of crowds with hands up, no phones visible. Festival energy, sunset, lights. "This is what you paid for."
🏷️ Tags: #festival #livemusic #aesthetic #handsup

## Week 2 — Solution Introduction
*Hook: introduce Handsup naturally, show the product in action*

**Day 6 — Monday**
🎬 Format: App demo / screen record
📝 Hook: *"Someone uploaded 47 clips from Laneway Melbourne and they're all free to download 👀"*
💡 Content: Screen record of searching "Tame Impala" on Handsup, finding clips, downloading one. Real product demo, no voiceover needed — just the UI.
🏷️ Tags: #laneway #tameimpala #festivalapp #handsup

**Day 7 — Tuesday**
🎬 Format: Storytelling
📝 Hook: *"We built an app that tells you to put your phone down. Then marketed it on TikTok."*
💡 Content: The origin story. The irony. Lean into it. "Look, we know. But also — someone at that show you're about to film? They're uploading to Handsup. So maybe you don't have to."
🏷️ Tags: #startup #app #festival #irony

**Day 8 — Wednesday**
🎬 Format: Before/after
📝 Hook: *"Me at Splendour 2022 vs me at Splendour 2025"*
💡 Content: 2022 — phone out the whole time. 2025 — hands up, downloading the clips after on Handsup. "The difference is embarrassing."
🏷️ Tags: #splendour #festival #glow #handsup

**Day 9 — Thursday**
🎬 Format: POV
📝 Hook: *"POV: You just found footage of yourself in the crowd from a show you went to 2 years ago"*
💡 Content: Recreate the moment of discovering yourself in someone else's clip. Emotional. Funny. Real.
🏷️ Tags: #festival #memories #fypシ #handsup

**Day 10 — Friday**
🎬 Format: Trend / audio
📝 Hook: *"Handsup is the app version of 'I'll take the photo so you can be in it'"*
💡 Content: Simple concept. Someone always has to be the one filming. What if that person uploaded it for everyone after? That's Handsup.
🏷️ Tags: #app #festival #concert #foryoupage

## Engagement Rules

- Reply to every comment in the first hour (signals to algorithm)
- Ask a question in every caption
- Use 3–5 hashtags max on TikTok (not 30)
- Post between 6–9pm Melbourne time for best AU reach
- Cross-post to Instagram Reels within 24 hours (no TikTok watermark — use SnapTik to remove)

---

## Content Creation Tools Needed

- iPhone or good Android (existing footage + screen recordings are fine for MVP content)
- CapCut (free, best for TikTok-style edits)
- Canva (for stat cards, text overlays, branded assets)
- Later or Buffer (for scheduling cross-platform)
