# 🙌 Handsup — Brand Guidelines

> Version 1.0 | March 2026

---

## 1. Identity

### Name
**handsup** — always lowercase in running text and the wordmark. Title case (Handsup) is acceptable in formal contexts (legal, press releases).

### Tagline
> **Be there. We'll film it.**

Short variant: **Be there.**

### What we are
A platform for music festival goers to share and discover video clips from live events. The product exists so people can be present — hands up, phone down — while still having the memory.

---

## 2. Logo

### Primary logo
Two raised hands + wordmark "handsup" + optional tagline beneath.

**Files:**
- `assets/logo.svg` — Full logo with tagline (for web, print, partnerships)
- `assets/icon.svg` — App icon version (hands only, no wordmark — for iOS/Android)

### Usage rules
- ✅ Logo on dark backgrounds (#0D0D0D, #111, deep purple)
- ✅ White wordmark on any dark surface
- ✅ Purple hands on dark backgrounds
- ❌ Never place logo on white or light backgrounds (kills the energy)
- ❌ Never stretch, rotate, or recolour the hands
- ❌ Never use the tagline without the wordmark

### Clear space
Minimum padding around the logo = height of the lowercase "h" in the wordmark on all sides.

## 4. Typography

### Font stack
**Primary:** `"SF Pro Display"` (iOS), `"Roboto"` (Android), fallback: `"Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif`

No custom font purchase needed — system fonts look excellent and load instantly.

### Type scale

| Role | Size | Weight | Colour |
|---|---|---|---|
| App name / Hero | 26–32px | 800 (ExtraBold) | White |
| Section heading | 18–20px | 700 (Bold) | White |
| Card title (artist) | 16–18px | 700 | White |
| Subheading (festival) | 13–15px | 600 | Electric Purple |
| Body text | 13–15px | 400 | `#AAAAAA` |
| Meta / Labels | 11–13px | 400–600 | `#555555` – `#666666` |
| Tagline / Caps | 12–14px | 400–600 | Electric Purple, letter-spacing: 2–3px |

### Voice in text
- Always lowercase for brand name: **handsup**
- CTA text: short, present tense — "Download", "Upload & share", "Be there"
- Avoid corporate language — no "leverage", "seamless", "solution"
- Conversational, festival-native — speak like a mate who was at the show

## 6. Motion & Feel

### Principles
- **Dark, warm, intimate** — like being inside a venue at night
- **Not cold, not corporate** — this is a human product about human moments
- **Energy without chaos** — fast enough to feel alive, not so fast it's exhausting

### Transitions
- Screen transitions: slide horizontal (standard navigation)
- Cards: fade in on scroll (subtle, 200ms)
- Download button: satisfying state change — purple → dark green + checkmark

### Glow effects
Use sparingly. A soft radial purple glow behind key UI elements (hero cards, active tabs) reinforces the "stage lights" aesthetic without being garish.

---

## 7. Photography & Video Thumbnails

Since content is user-generated, the UI should make even average footage look great:
- Cards use rounded corners (16px) to soften rough edges
- Gradient overlays on thumbnails (bottom: rgba(0,0,0,0.6)) to ensure text legibility
- Aspect ratio: **16:9** for all clip thumbnails

---

## 8. Tone of Voice

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Onboarding | Warm, exciting | "This is your first festival. Put the phone down." |
| Upload success | Celebratory | "Video uploaded! 🙌 Thanks for sharing the moment." |
| Empty state | Encouraging | "No clips yet. Be the first to upload one." |
| Download | Satisfying | "Saved to your camera roll." |
| Error | Human, calm | "Something went wrong. Try again?" |

---

## 9. What Handsup Is Not

- ❌ Not a video editing app (no filters, no effects — raw and real)
- ❌ Not a social network (no followers, likes, or clout mechanics in V1)
- ❌ Not Instagram or TikTok (no algorithm-driven infinite scroll — intent-based search)
- ❌ Not corporate or polished — real footage from real people at real shows
