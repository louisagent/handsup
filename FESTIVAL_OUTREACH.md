# 🎪 Handsup — Australian Festival Outreach Target List

> Research compiled: March 2026
> Purpose: Festival partnership outreach for Handsup pilot season
> Priority: Top 3 are first-contact targets. Remainder are second-wave.

---

## Priority Tier 1 — First Contact

These are the highest-fit targets: community-driven, social-savvy, boutique crowds. One yes here seeds the platform.

---

### 1. Laneway Festival 🏆 *#1 Target*

**Why:** Boutique, Australian-owned, intensely community-driven. Exactly the crowd that cares about live music culture. National footprint (Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Adelaide, Fremantle).

**Contacts:**
- **Partnerships/Sponsorship:** darby@aeroplaneagency.com *(via Aeroplane Agency, Laneway's sponsorship rep)*
- **General:** info@lanewayfestival.com.au
- **Website:** lanewayfestival.com

**Approach:** Lead with the community angle — Handsup makes their attendees' footage findable and gives Laneway an engagement layer post-event. Offer the first year free with branded event pages.

**Email subject line idea:**
> "A new way for Laneway attendees to relive the weekend — and for you to see what they captured"

---

### 2. Field Day (Sydney)

**Why:** New Year's Day, highly social crowd, strong social media presence. Promoted by Fuzzy Operations — if Fuzzy likes it, they also run Listen Out (double the opportunity).

**Contacts:**
- **Sponsorship/Partnerships:** sponsorship@fuzzy.com.au *(Fuzzy Operations — runs both Field Day and Listen Out)*
- **General:** info@fuzzy.com.au
- **Website:** fieldday.com.au

**Note:** One email to Fuzzy could open doors to both Field Day and Listen Out simultaneously. Lead with Field Day, mention Listen Out as a natural expansion.

**Email subject line idea:**
> "What if Field Day attendees could find and download their footage from the day?"

## Outreach Template

*Adapt this for each festival — don't send the same email twice.*

```
Subject: A platform for [Festival Name] footage — worth 5 minutes?

Hi [Name],

I'm building Handsup — an app where festival attendees can find and 
download video clips from events they attended, searchable by artist, 
stage, and date.

The idea: people put their phones down and actually watch the show, 
knowing the footage exists and they can find it after.

I'd love [Festival Name] to be our launch partner. What that means 
for you: a branded event page, aggregate clip analytics (which sets 
got captured the most, how far the content travelled), and a new 
post-event engagement channel. Free for your first event.

Would you be open to a 20-minute call to see if it's a fit?

[Your name]
handsup.app | hello@handsup.app
```
