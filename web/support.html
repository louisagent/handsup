Frequently asked questions

How do I find clips from a specific festival?
Tap the Search tab and type the artist name, festival name, city, or date. Results update as you type. You can also browse by festival in the Events tab — tap any festival to see all uploaded clips.
How do I download a video?
Open any clip and tap "Download to phone". The video saves directly to your camera roll. You'll need to grant Handsup permission to access your photos the first time.
How do I upload a clip?
Tap the Upload tab (🙌 in the bottom bar). Choose a video from your library, fill in the artist name, festival, location, and date — then tap Upload & share. Your clip will be live immediately.
Is Handsup free?
Yes. Uploading is free. Downloading is free. No subscription required. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core experience will always be free.
Can I upload footage of any artist?
Yes — as long as you filmed it yourself at a live event. Don't upload content you didn't capture, official broadcast recordings, or anything you don't have the right to share. Rights holders can request removal at any time.
Someone uploaded a clip of me without my consent. What do I do?
Email us at hello@handsuplive.com with the clip link and we'll remove it within 24 hours.
I represent an artist or label — how do I request a takedown?
Email dmca@handsuplive.com with the clip URL and your authorisation details. We process all takedown requests within 48 hours.
How do creator rewards work?
Top uploaders earn reputation points based on their clip quality, download counts, and community engagement. High-reputation uploaders get access to rewards including festival tickets, merch, and early features. The full rewards programme is coming soon.
I'm a festival organiser — can we partner with Handsup?
Yes! We'd love to talk. Email festivals@handsuplive.com and we'll send through our partnership deck.
The app isn't working — what should I do?
Try force-closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, check you're on the latest version. Still stuck? Email us at hello@handsuplive.com with your device type, OS version, and a description of the issue.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile → Settings → Delete account. Your personal data will be removed within 30 days. If you need help, email hello@handsuplive.com.
