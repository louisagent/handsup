Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Handsup ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and a username you choose.

Content you upload: Videos, titles, tags (artist, festival, location, date), and descriptions you provide when uploading clips.

Usage data: Which clips you view, search, download, or save. We use this to improve search relevance and show trending content.

Device information: Basic device type and OS version, used for debugging and app compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Handsup service — search, upload, download clips
  • To personalise your experience (e.g. suggestions based on artists you've searched)
  • To send notifications you've opted into (new clips, trending sets, etc.)
  • To improve the app and fix bugs
  • To detect and prevent abuse or spam

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data to serve third-party advertising.

3. What We Share

Your public profile (username, upload history) is visible to other users.

Your download history is private — only you can see what you've downloaded.

We share data with third-party services only as needed to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — database and video storage (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Expo / React Native — app delivery infrastructure

4. Video Content & Copyright

By uploading a video, you confirm you have the right to share it. You retain ownership of content you upload. You grant Handsup a licence to store, display, and make it available for download by other users.

If you believe content infringes your copyright, contact us at dmca@handsuplive.com. We will review and remove content promptly in line with DMCA and Australian copyright law.

5. Data Retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days. Uploaded clips may remain if others have downloaded them — contact us to request full removal.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@handsuplive.com.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted data storage, HTTPS, and access controls. No system is 100% secure — if you discover a vulnerability, please disclose it responsibly to hello@handsuplive.com.

8. Children

Handsup is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we'll remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@handsuplive.com — we'll respond within 48 hours.
