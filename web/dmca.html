Copyright & DMCA

Last updated: March 2026

⚡ 48-hour response commitment

Handsup respects intellectual property rights. All content on the platform is user-uploaded footage filmed by attendees at live events. If you believe any content infringes your copyright or other intellectual property rights, please submit a takedown request below.

🎥 About our content: Handsup hosts short clips (max 60 seconds) filmed by concert and festival attendees. Uploaders confirm they personally filmed the footage and accept liability for any infringement. We enforce upload limits and respond promptly to all valid takedown requests.

What Happens After You Submit

  • Within 48 hours: We review your request and contact you to confirm receipt
  • Valid requests: The content is removed and the uploader is notified
  • Repeat infringers: Accounts are permanently terminated
  • Invalid or incomplete requests: We'll contact you for clarification

Counter-Notices

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notice to dmca@handsuplive.com. Your counter-notice must include:

  • Your name and contact details
  • The URL of the removed content
  • A statement explaining why you believe the removal was in error
  • A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of Victorian courts (Australia)
  • Your electronic signature

Upon receiving a valid counter-notice, we will notify the original complainant. If they do not file a court action within 10 business days, the content may be reinstated at our discretion.

Abuse of the Process

Submitting false takedown requests is a serious matter. Under Australian law and internationally, knowingly misrepresenting that content is infringing can expose you to liability. We reserve the right to take action against bad-faith complainants.

Our Platform Policy

Handsup proactively works to prevent copyright infringement through:

  • Upload limits: 60-second maximum clip length; 10 uploads per user per event
  • Uploader declarations: All uploaders confirm they personally filmed the footage and accept liability
  • Prompt takedowns: 48-hour response to all valid requests
  • Account termination: Repeat infringers are permanently banned

We believe the best outcome is one where festival footage serves fans, artists, and the live music industry — not one that harms anyone's rights. We're always open to constructive conversations with rights holders.

For anything other than takedown requests, email hello@handsuplive.com.
