# 🙌 Handsup — 30-Day Social Media Content Calendar

> Launch month content plan for TikTok + Instagram Reels
> Platform: TikTok (primary) + Instagram Reels (mirrored same day)
> Cadence: 3x per week (Mon / Wed / Fri)
> Tone: Relatable, festival-native, self-aware. Not corporate. Not polished.
> Core angle: *"An app that helps you not use your phone at concerts — marketed on TikTok."*

---

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | % of Content | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **The Irony** | 35% | Leans into the "phone-free app on TikTok" joke. Shareable, funny. |
| **The Moment** | 25% | Emotional content — being present, the feeling of live music |
| **Social Proof** | 20% | Reactions, discoveries, real footage of the product working |
| **Education** | 15% | How Handsup works, why it exists |
| **Community** | 5% | Behind the scenes, founding story, early users |

## Week 1 — Launch Week: Introduce the Concept

*Goal: establish the irony hook, get the first wave of followers who "get it"*

---

### Day 1 — Monday (Launch)

**Pillar:** The Irony
**Format:** Talking head / text overlay

**Hook:** *"I built an app that tells you to put your phone down at concerts. Here's why I'm on TikTok."*

**Script:**
> "So I made an app. It helps you stop filming at music festivals and actually enjoy the show. [pause] And now I'm here. On TikTok. Marketing it. [pause] Anyway — here's how it works."
> Brief demo of searching for a festival, finding clips, downloading one.
> "Someone else filmed it. Now it's yours. You're welcome. Link in bio."

**CTA:** "Follow if you've ever regretted filming instead of watching"

---

### Day 2 — Wednesday

**Pillar:** The Moment
**Format:** Montage / voiceover

**Hook:** *"The best concert moments aren't filmed. They're felt."*

**Script:**
> Voiceover over slow-mo crowd footage:
> "You know the feeling. The drop hits. The lights go. Everyone goes up at once. And for one second — nobody's looking at a screen. [pause] That's what we're trying to protect. And yeah — we built an app to do it."
> End card: handsup logo + "Be there. We'll film it."

**CTA:** "Tag someone you've been to a festival with"

---

### Day 3 — Friday

**Pillar:** Education
**Format:** Screen recording walkthrough

**Hook:** *"How do you find footage from a festival you went to?"*

**Script:**
> "Someone asked me this. Here's how it works on Handsup."
> Screen record: open app → search "Tame Impala Melbourne" → clips appear → tap → download
> "That's it. No account needed to browse. Takes 10 seconds."
> "And if you filmed something? Upload it. Tag it. Someone will be searching for it tonight."

**CTA:** "What festival are you searching for first?"

## Week 2 — Build the Narrative

*Goal: introduce the "phone culture at festivals" conversation. Get people talking.*

---

### Day 4 — Monday

**Pillar:** The Irony
**Format:** Duet bait / reaction setup

**Hook:** *"Festival footage be like:"*

**Script:**
> Two-panel split: left side — shaky, dark, barely audible phone footage. Right side — same moment, clear, from someone with a steadier hand who uploaded to Handsup.
> Text overlay: "One of these is what you filmed. One is what you could've downloaded."
> End: "handsup.app — someone else has the shot."

**CTA:** "Which one is your footage? 😭"

---

### Day 5 — Wednesday

**Pillar:** The Moment
**Format:** Talking head

**Hook:** *"I asked 10 festival goers what they regret most. Every single one said the same thing."*

**Script:**
> "Filming. They all said filming. [pause] Specifically — filming instead of watching. [pause] So I built the thing that means you don't have to choose anymore."
> Hold on camera. Let it sit.
> "Someone else is always filming. Go find their clip. Go enjoy the show."

**CTA:** "What's the last show you actually watched instead of filmed?"

---

### Day 6 — Friday

**Pillar:** Social Proof
**Format:** POV / reaction

**Hook:** *"POV: You searched your favourite artist on Handsup and found THIS"*

**Script:**
> Screen recording of a search pulling up a real (or mock) set of clips.
> Reaction overlay: genuine surprise face.
> "Okay the fact that someone uploaded the FULL Flume set from Field Day. I'm actually—"
> Download. Save to camera roll.
> "Link in bio. Go find your set."

**CTA:** "What artist are you searching first? Drop it below 👇"

## Evergreen Content (Use Any Week as Fill)

These can be deployed any time to fill gaps or respond to trending audio.

---

**E1 — "Name a festival where you put your phone away"**
Simple prompt. Generates comments. High engagement.

**E2 — "The best concert you ever attended, described in 3 words"**
Same mechanic. Position Handsup as aligned with that vibe.

**E3 — "Rating festivals by how phone-free their crowd is"**
Laneway: 8/10. Meredith: 10/10. Coachella: 3/10. Etc.
Funny, shareable, debate-baiting.

**E4 — Behind the scenes: building Handsup**
Screen share of the app being built. Devlog style. Niche but loyal audience.

**E5 — "Search [current trending festival] on Handsup right now"**
React to whatever festival is happening that week. Timely. Easy to make.

**E6 — Trending audio remount**
Take any trending TikTok audio and write a Handsup-specific script over it. Fast to produce, high discovery potential.

## Notes for Execution

- **Trending audio:** Check TikTok Creative Centre weekly. Swap audio on any post if a trend fits.
- **Comments:** Reply to every comment in the first hour. TikTok's algo rewards comment velocity.
- **Reels:** Post to Instagram within 24h of TikTok. Don't cross-post with TikTok watermark — re-export clean.
- **Pinned posts:** Pin Day 1 (launch), Day 8 (founder story), and your best-performing clip.
- **Stories:** Mirror key posts to Instagram Stories with a poll or question sticker for extra engagement.
- **Don't delete:** Low-performing posts sometimes blow up 2 weeks later. Leave everything up.
