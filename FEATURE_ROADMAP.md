# Handsup — Feature Roadmap

> Ideas and future features. Prioritised by impact.

---

## ✅ Completed (v1.1)

### Social Groups
- ✅ **Add friends** — find and follow other users
- ✅ **Festival Groups** — create a group with people you went to an event with
  - All members upload their clips into the shared group
  - Group feed shows everyone's footage from that night
  - Private groups (invite-only) for personal/friend use
  - Public groups for open community events

### Sharing
- ✅ **Share to Instagram** — share clips directly to Stories/Reels
- ✅ **In-app sharing** — share clip links with friends
- ✅ **Group invites** — invite friends to a group via link or username

### Content Organization
- ✅ **Collaborative playlists** — organize clips into custom collections
- ✅ **Download packs** — bulk download all clips from groups/events/playlists

### Social Features
- ✅ Push notifications (friend uploads, group activity)
- ✅ Comments on clips
- ✅ Creator badges and verified uploaders

---

## 🔜 Next Up (v1.2)

### Creator Tools
- **Festival partner dashboards** — analytics for partner festivals
- **Artist pages** — enhanced pages with tour dates, streaming links
- **Creator insights** — advanced analytics (demographics, retention, etc.)

### Discovery
- **Algorithmic recommendations** — ML-powered "For You" feed
- **Trending sounds** — track audio used across clips
- **Similar clips** — find more clips like this one

### Monetization
- **Tip jar** — support creators directly
- **Premium features** — ad-free, early access, exclusive content
- **Festival tickets** — buy tickets directly in-app

---

## 🔮 Future (v1.3+)

### Social
- Live streaming from festivals
- Stories (24h ephemeral clips)
- Reactions/emoji responses on clips
- Voice notes in comments

### Content
- Multi-angle sync (sync clips from same moment)
- AI highlight reels (auto-generate best moments)
- Photo mode (not just video)
- 360° video support

### Events
- Festival schedules/set times
- Meet-up spots / friend finder at events
- Check-in system
- Weather & logistics info

---

## 💡 Notes

- Groups are the core social loop: go to a festival → create a group → invite your mates → everyone uploads → you have a full multi-angle archive of your night
- Instagram integration is high priority for virality — clips shared to Stories drive app downloads
- Private groups make it personal and sticky (your friend group's private archive)
