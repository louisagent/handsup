## 1. Laneway Festival — Partnership Pitch

**To:** partnerships@laneway.com.au (or the contact form at lanewayfestival.com)
**Subject:** Partnership idea — turning your crowd into a content library

---

Hi Laneway team,

I'm the founder of Handsup — a platform we're building specifically for music festival goers.

The idea is simple: people put their phones down and enjoy the show, because they know they can search for footage afterwards. Attendees upload clips tagged by artist, festival, and date. Everyone else finds and downloads them later.

We're launching soon and want Laneway to be our first Australian festival partner.

Here's what that looks like in practice:

**What you get (free for year one):**
- A branded Laneway event page on Handsup, for every event
- Aggregate analytics on which artists generated the most content, engagement, and downloads
- A verified uploader credential for any official Laneway photographers or crew
- Featured placement as a "Launch Partner" in the app and on our website
- A post-event content library your team can browse and use for social

**What we ask:**
- A mention to your attendees — one line in your post-event email: "Find footage from the weekend on Handsup"
- That's genuinely it for the first year

The pitch to your audience writes itself: *"Put your phone down — Handsup has the footage."* It aligns with where concert culture is heading (Yondr, phone-free sets, presence over performance), and it's something no other festival is offering yet.

I'd love 20 minutes to show you the app and talk through what a pilot could look like before your next event.

Happy to jump on a call whenever suits.

[Your name]
[Your phone]
hello@handsup.app
