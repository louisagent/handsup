# Handsup — Design Spec

> For designers, AI image tools, or anyone producing visual assets for Handsup.

---

## Logo Brief (for Midjourney / DALL-E / Canva / designer)

```
App logo for "handsup" — a music festival video sharing platform.

Visual: Two stylised hands raised in the air (concert crowd gesture), 
rendered in geometric/minimal style. NOT realistic hands — clean, 
iconic shapes like you'd see in a modern app icon.

Colour: Electric purple (#8B5CF6) to light lavender (#C4B5FD) gradient 
on the hands. White wordmark "handsup" in bold lowercase sans-serif beneath.

Background: Near-black (#0D0D0D). Optional: very subtle radial purple glow 
emanating from between the hands, like stage lighting.

Style: Modern, minimal, premium. Think Spotify meets a festival wristband.
No photography, no gradients on the wordmark, no drop shadows.

Aspect ratio: 1:1 (app icon format)
```

---

## Splash Screen Brief

```
Handsup app splash/loading screen.

Full dark background (#0D0D0D). 
Centred: the handsup logo (hands + wordmark).
Below wordmark: tagline "BE THERE. WE'LL FILM IT." in electric purple, 
all caps, wide letter-spacing.

Atmosphere: Large radial glow in deep purple (#8B5CF6 at 20% opacity) 
behind the logo, like stage lights in a dark venue.

Bottom of screen: subtle crowd silhouette (solid dark purple, barely visible).
```

---

## Social / Marketing Asset Brief

```
Promotional image for handsup festival app.

Scene: Outdoor music festival at night, crowd with hands raised.
NO PHONES visible in the crowd — this is intentional and on-brand.
Stage lights in purple and white illuminating the scene.

Overlay: handsup logo top-left. Tagline "BE THERE. WE'LL FILM IT." 
centred in large bold white text.

Colour grade: Cool, purple-tinted night scene. High contrast.
Cinematic, 16:9 aspect ratio.
```
